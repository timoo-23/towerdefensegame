The city used to be loud.

Cars. People. Music. Life.

Now... it's just silence.

...

You wake up in the middle of an abandoned street.

The air feels heavy.

Something is wrong.

...

A voice crackles through your radio.

"Hey... if you can hear me, stay alive."

"Those things roaming around... they’re not human anymore."

"They’re slow, but they don’t stop."

...

You look around.

Shadows move in the distance.

Zombies.

...

"Listen carefully," the voice continues.

"You need to survive waves of them."

"They’ll keep coming... one after another."

...

"You can move using W A S D."

"Aim with your mouse."

"Shoot them before they reach you."

"If they get too close... you’ll take damage."

...

"Ammo is limited. Reload when you need to."

"And don’t panic."

...

"After each wave, you’ll get time to prepare."

"Use that time wisely."

"Upgrade yourself. Get stronger."

...

The radio goes quiet.

...

The wind howls through the empty buildings.

You tighten your grip.

...

"Alright..."

"Let’s survive."
